**Key Technical Concepts**
-   **Hybrid Data Fetcher**: A service that abstracts data sourcing, checking a cache first, then attempting to fetch from a primary source (IB, when connected) before falling back to a secondary source (Alpaca).
-   **Market-Wide Backtesting**: The ability to run a trading strategy against a large universe of symbols over a historical period to find all potential trades and aggregate performance.
-   **Regime-Aware Trading**: A system where bot behavior (like position sizing) and performance analysis are dynamically adjusted based on the broader market condition (e.g., Bull, Bear, Sideways).
-   **Background Task Architecture**: Services like the Market Scanner are designed as background tasks with status-checking endpoints to handle long-running operations without blocking the UI.
