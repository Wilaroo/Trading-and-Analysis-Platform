### 2. 🔴 Silent-orphan adoption — CEG +161 long, FIG -2,496 short
Fills hit IB but never wrote to `bot_trades`. Reconciler `v19_34_15b` later adopted them
("untracked_position" → adopted as AMBER without brackets). $100K untracked exposure on
$237K account at peak (~42%).

**Root cause hypothesis:** order submitted → IB filled → exception/timeout between fill
confirmation and DB write → bot moves on → IB has position bot doesn't know about.

**Forensics path:** `~/sentcom-debug-2026-05-11/order-queue.json` shows fill records;
cross-reference with `bot_trades` collection to find the gap.

### 3. 🔴 NBIS retry-on-cap-rejection
`safety/status.recent_checks` shows symbol_exposure cap correctly blocked NBIS 4 times at
$100K notional. But bot retried with smaller sizes and got fills (405 + 193 + 162 shares).
Then `external_close_v19_34_15b` closed each as "phantom" — losing -$326 real money on
real fills.

**Fix:** when symbol_exposure cap blocks → enter symbol cooldown (e.g. 5 min), don't retry
smaller.

### 4. 🔴 `external_close_v19_34_15b` falsely closes trades on view-mismatch
Reconciler currently marks bot's trade "externally closed" whenever bot view != IB view.
Should: only close on IB position transitioning from non-zero → zero with a confirmed
fill event. Otherwise we create accounting phantoms from real fills.

### 5. 🟡 Brackets not auto-applied on adoption
When reconciler adopts untracked positions (CEG/FIG), they come in without SL/PT.
Defenseless real positions. Need: compute default bracket (e.g. 2 ATR stop, 1.5R target)
at adoption time and submit.
